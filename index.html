⚠️ SAFETY TIPS

🌸 FLOWER POTS & FOUNTAINS

  • ✗Do not light keeping face above the flower pots
  • ✗Do not hold on hand during or after lighting
  • ✗Do not use candle or safety matches to light
  • ✓Light fuse with lengthy sparklers & get away immediately

🚀 FANCY AERIAL GRAND REPEATERS

  • ✗Do not use match stick or naked flame to ignite
  • ✗Keep 200 metres clear of people around
  • ✓Place on level surface, remove paper belt & straighten fuse
  • ✓Use lengthy lighted sparklers, get away to 16 metres

🔥 General Safety: Always burst crackers in open spaces away from buildings & trees. Keep a bucket of water or sand nearby. Never allow children to burst crackers alone. Wear cotton clothes. Keep pets indoors. When in doubt, don't light it!
